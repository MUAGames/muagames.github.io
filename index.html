New Site

The site finally has a new look! The old site was great, but after 7 years it was getting a little stale and out of date. Now we have a new look, a much better stack, and better hosting – basically an upgrade all around.

The old tutorials have been copied over (although they are definitely showing their age) but most of the extra junk has been removed. For now, this means the site will be a little sparse, especially since all the MUA time available in the near future is going into games. Don’t fret, though - more tutorials, libraries, and games are on the horizon (although with indefinite timelines).

For now, enjoy the minimal content, email us if you have anything to ask or say, and keep making games!
